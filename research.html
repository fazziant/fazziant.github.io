Hi there!

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Department ELEC, Vrije Universiteit Brussel (VUB). My main scientific interests lie in numerical linear algebra and scientific computing with applications in system and control theory.

A list on my main research topics follows:
  • Matrix perturbation
  • Structured low-rank approximation
  • Approximate GCD
  • System identification
  • Matrix analysis
  • Least squares fitting
  • Numerical optimization
  • Behavioral approach
  • Matrix polynomials
